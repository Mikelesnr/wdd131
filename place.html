About Harare

Harare is the capital of Zimbabwe and my hometown. Known for its vibrant culture and bustling markets, Harare is a city that beautifully blends modernity with rich traditions. The city’s lush gardens, such as the Harare Gardens and the National Botanic Garden, offer serene escapes amidst the urban landscape. Harare is also a hub of economic activity, with a thriving arts scene and numerous historical landmarks that tell the story of Zimbabwe’s past. Whether you’re exploring the bustling streets or enjoying the peaceful parks, Harare has something unique to offer everyone.
